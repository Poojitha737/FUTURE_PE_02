# Script A – The Weekend Hangout Problem

**Platform:** Instagram Reels  
**Duration:** 30 seconds  
**Format:** Voiceover / talking to camera  
**Hook Type:** Relatable problem  

---

## Script

**[Hook — 0–3s]**  
"Okay so every weekend my friends and I have this same argument — where do we go in Vizag that actually feels different?"

**[Problem — 3–8s]**  
"Most cafes here are either too crowded, too pricey, or just... boring. We wanted a vibe. Something unique."

**[Discovery — 8–18s]**  
"Then someone dragged us to Area 51 in Rushikonda. And honestly? I was not ready. Superbikes parked outside, board games inside, mocktails on the table — all under ₹450 for two people."

**[Experience — 18–25s]**  
"We ended up playing billiards for an hour, tried the Peri Peri Fries — lowkey the best thing I've eaten this month — and didn't want to leave."

**[CTA — 25–30s]**  
"If you're in Vizag and bored of the usual spots, go. Link in bio for location."

---

## Production Notes
- Shoot handheld for authentic feel — avoid gimbal
- B-roll: entrance with superbikes, board games on table, hands playing billiards, Peri Peri Fries close-up
- Trending audio: use a calm lo-fi or a currently trending Reels sound
- Subtitles on screen throughout (accessibility + silent viewers)
