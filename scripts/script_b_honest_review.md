# Script B – The Honest Review

**Platform:** YouTube Shorts  
**Duration:** 45 seconds  
**Format:** Direct-to-camera review  
**Hook Type:** Credibility / review format  

---

## Script

**[Hook — 0–4s]**  
"I went to the most secretive cafe in Visakhapatnam so you don't have to guess if it's worth it."

**[Setup — 4–12s]**  
"Area 51 — The Cafe Hideout. It's tucked near Rushikonda IT SEZ, up a hill, and honestly feels like you found a secret base. They lean into that theme hard."

**[The Good — 12–28s]**  
"Atmosphere? Solid 5/5. Warm lighting, board games, billiards table, and the energy is just... chill. Not trying too hard. We had the Peri Peri Fries and mocha — both genuinely good. Price for two? Around ₹450. That's wild value."

**[The Honest Part — 28–38s]**  
"The washrooms could use work — I'll be real about that. And some menu items are hit or miss. But for the vibe and games? We stayed way longer than planned."

**[CTA — 38–45s]**  
"Area 51 Cafe, Rushikonda, Vizag. Drop it in your location list. Comment if you've been — what's your go-to order?"

---

## Production Notes
- Talk directly to camera — no fancy setup needed, phone is fine
- Be comfortable with pauses; they feel more authentic
- B-roll: walk-in shot from outside, wide shot of interior, close-up of food
- End card: show Google Maps pin or Instagram handle
- Honest critique (washrooms) actually builds credibility — keep it in
