## CTA Strategy Notes

- **Engagement CTAs** (soft, question, social): Best for organic content — drives comments and shares
- **Conversion CTAs** (FOMO, direct, location): Best for paid Instagram ads — drives visits
- **Avoid:** "Click the link below", "Follow us", "Check our bio" — too generic, feels like an ad
- **Best combination:** End script with a location CTA, end caption with a question CTA
