# Prompt: Caption + Hashtag Generator

## Purpose
Generate platform-ready Instagram captions in two tones — discovery and honest review.

## Prompt Template

```
Write 2 Instagram captions for [CAFE NAME] in [CITY] for a UGC-style post.

Caption 1 — Discovery tone: Excited, personal, feels like a real visit from a friend.
Caption 2 — Honest review tone: Balanced, authentic, mentions one thing that could 
be better alongside the positives.

End each caption with 8–10 relevant local hashtags mixing:
- Cafe-specific tags (#[CafeName]Vizag)
- City food tags (#VizagFoodie, #VisakhapatnamCafe)
- Niche tags (#HangoutVizag, #CafeVibes)

Max 150 words per caption.
Do not use "amazing", "wonderful", "must visit", or any generic influencer phrases.
```
